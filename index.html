DataHammer BETA



When you sell your phone, be sure to protect your privacy.
  ★ Run the recommended factory reset.
  ★ Install and run Data Hammer.


Data Hammer overwrites all your unused storage with random data, so it can't be undeleted.

This program runs on KitKat 4.4.4, with both Dalvik and ART operating systems. It will take about 1-2 minutes per gb of free data - that's about 10 minutes on my Nexus4. During that time, it is best not to use your phone.



Data Hammer is free and open source.

Please report all issues with Data Hammer https://github.com/darkoverlordofdata/datahammer/issues
